# KKK / FAQ

- Kuller leitakse tavaliselt 1–2 minutiga / A courier is typically found within 1–2 minutes
- Makse toimub automaatselt äpi kaudu pärast tarnet / Payment is processed automatically through the app after delivery
- Kulleriks saamine: registreeru äpis → dokumendid → verifitseerimine (kuni 24h) / To become a courier: register in app → upload documents → verification (up to 24h)
- Kõik saadetised on veo ajal kindlustatud (täpsemalt kasutustingimustes) / All shipments are insured during transit (see terms of service for details)
